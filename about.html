About Me

I am Ashutosh Mukherjee and I am an aspiring Mechanical Engineer. I have graduated from Punjab Engineering College, Chandigarh in 2020 and am currently pursuing my masters degree from RWTH Aachen University.

I have keen interests in the field of Dynamics and Control. After my bachelors, I worked in a research project associated with DRDO (Defence Research and Development Organisation, India). The project was related to the dynamic modelling and control system design of a lower limb strength augmentation exoskeleton.

I also have experience as a Teaching Assistant at Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology, where I was involved in a graduate level course (Modern Control of Dynamic systems) and an undergraduate level course (Mechatronic Systems Lab).
